What is Cricket?

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It originated in England and has become a global sport.

Cricket Formats

There are three main formats of cricket:

  • Test Cricket
  • One Day Internationals (ODIs)
  • T20 Cricket
